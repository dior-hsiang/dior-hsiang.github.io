Frequently Asked Questions

Discover the world of SUPER DIOR, where your luxury experience is our top priority. Below are answers to some of the most common questions.

How do I schedule a personalized consultation with a SUPER DIOR stylist?

You can easily schedule a private consultation by contacting our concierge team through your account or by reaching out via email. Our stylists will work with you to craft a unique look tailored to your preferences.

Do you offer custom-made clothing or accessories?

Yes, every piece from SUPER DIOR can be customized to reflect your personal taste. Our master tailors and designers offer a bespoke service to create truly unique fashion items that perfectly suit your style.

What benefits come with becoming a VIP member of SUPER DIOR?

As a VIP member, you will receive exclusive access to limited-edition collections, personalized services, invites to private events, and much more. Your membership ensures you receive the highest level of service, including a dedicated concierge available 24/7.

Can I request a private shopping experience?

Absolutely. Our private shopping experiences are designed for those who seek a more personal, intimate experience. Our team will guide you through the collection in a luxurious setting, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction throughout your visit.

How can I stay informed about new collections and exclusive offers?

By subscribing to our newsletter or becoming a member of SUPER DIOR, you'll receive early access to new collections, special offers, and invitations to exclusive events. Stay connected with us to always be ahead of the trends.
